# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28.12.2025

## 1. Introduction

Recon Insight ("we", "our", or "the App") respects your privacy.  
This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and your rights when using the application.

By using Recon Insight, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

We do **not** collect or store personal data such as names, emails, passwords, or messages.

The App processes only:

- Publicly available in-game statistics
- Data retrieved via the Riot Games API
- Data obtained through Riot Games OAuth authentication

No sensitive or private user information is stored on our servers.

---

## 3. Use of Riot Games Services

Recon Insight uses:

- Riot Games OAuth for authentication
- Riot Games API to retrieve game statistics

All data usage complies with Riot Games’ Developer Policies.  
Recon Insight is **not affiliated with, endorsed, sponsored, or approved by Riot Games**.

---

## 4. How We Use Data

The collected data is used solely to:

- Display in-game statistics
- Provide insights and analytics related to gameplay

We do not sell, rent, or share user data with third parties.

---

## 5. Data Storage

Recon Insight does not permanently store personal user data.  
Game statistics may be cached temporarily to improve performance.

---

## 6. Cookies and Tracking

The mobile application does not use cookies or third-party tracking technologies.

If a website version is introduced in the future, this policy may be updated accordingly.

---

## 7. Children’s Privacy

Recon Insight is intended for users aged **13 and older**, in accordance with Riot Games’ policies.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

---

## 8. Data Accuracy Disclaimer

Game statistics are provided "as is" and may be incomplete, delayed, or inaccurate due to limitations of third-party APIs.

Recon Insight does not guarantee the accuracy or completeness of displayed data.
