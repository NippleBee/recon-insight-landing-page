# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28.12.2025

## 1. Introduction

Recon Insight ("we", "our", or "the App") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it.

This Privacy Policy explains what information is processed when you use Recon Insight, how it is used, and what rights you have.

By using the App, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

---

## 2. Information We Process

Recon Insight does **not** collect or store personal data such as:

- Real names
- Email addresses
- Passwords
- Phone numbers
- Messages or chats

The App processes only the following data:

- Publicly available in-game statistics
- Match and ranked data retrieved via the Riot Games API
- Basic account identifiers provided by Riot Games OAuth (such as PUUID)

We do **not** access or store Riot account credentials.

---

## 3. Riot Games OAuth & API Usage

Recon Insight uses:

- **Riot Games OAuth** for user authentication
- **Riot Games APIs** to retrieve game-related data

Data is accessed strictly in accordance with Riot Games Developer Policies.

Recon Insight is **not affiliated with, endorsed, sponsored, or approved by Riot Games**.

---

## 4. Purpose of Data Processing

Processed data is used exclusively to:

- Display match history and gameplay statistics
- Generate analytics and insights related to player performance
- Improve user experience and application performance

We do **not** use data for advertising, profiling, or marketing purposes.

---

## 5. Data Storage and Retention

- No personal user data is permanently stored
- Game statistics may be cached temporarily to improve performance and reduce API usage
- Cached data is automatically refreshed or removed after a short period

We do not retain data longer than necessary for the App’s functionality.

---

## 6. Data Sharing

Recon Insight does **not** sell, rent, or share user data with third parties.

Data is only exchanged with Riot Games services as required for authentication and statistics retrieval.

---

## 7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The mobile application does **not** use cookies or third-party tracking technologies.

If a web version is introduced in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

---

## 8. User Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union, you have the right to:

- Request access to processed data
- Request correction or deletion of cached data
- Withdraw consent by disconnecting your Riot account

Requests can be sent to: **support@recon-insight.xyz**

---

## 9. Children’s Privacy

Recon Insight is intended for users aged **13 years or older**, in accordance with Riot Games policies.

We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

---

## 10. Data Accuracy Disclaimer

All game statistics are provided "as is".

Data may be incomplete, delayed, or inaccurate due to limitations or changes in third-party APIs.

---

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

The updated version will be available within the App or on the official website, with the "Last updated" date revised accordingly.
